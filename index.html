Why Bittensor

Subnet vs. centralized pipeline

LLM extraction is probabilistic —
aggregating many outputs improves accuracy

Centralized

  • One internal team
  • Few extraction pipelines
  • Outputs vary across runs
  • Repeated adjudication = cost
  • Quality improvement bottlenecked

Claims Subnet

  • Many miners, diverse strategies
  • Validators compare & score
  • Competition improves signal
  • Graph improves over time
  • Quality improvement is incentive-aligned
